Net Metering — Key Facts
Net metering allows you to feed excess solar power back to the national grid and get credit on your electricity bill at a government-set buy-back rate.

Buy-back rate (May 2026): ~PKR 22–24/unit (varies by DISCO)
Eligibility: Any system 1kW–1MW. Must have on-grid or hybrid inverter.
Registration: Apply to your DISCO (LESCO, FESCO, GEPCO etc.). Process takes 1–3 months.
One-time cost: PKR 20,000–35,000 (DISCO processing + bi-directional meter)
Annual benefit: Can reduce or eliminate your WAPDA bill. Excess units carried forward monthly.
